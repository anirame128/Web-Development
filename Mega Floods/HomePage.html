Most cliffs that have been carved or eroded by water will have the shape of a horseshoe. One of the most known waterfalls in the world niagara falls has the same shape as cliffs in the scablands. For that reason this part of the scablands is called the dry falls. They are five times the size of niagara and almost twice as tall.

Another clue also lies here in the scablands. Many miles away from the drie falls are strange super sized sinkholes. Sinkholes are many times found under fast going rivers. This can only point to one thing that a giant force of water carved this ripped apart landscape. Something very mysterious is that there is not a single lake or river the size and proximity away from this area to suit a flood like this. To get this type of formation a huge amount of water must be in the area.

They are so similar that people suspect they were made in the same way. To find out how long it took to carve these magnificent landscapes scientists are using a method called surface exposure dating. Earths surface rocks are constantly bombarded by cosmic rays from space. Cosmic rays are proton particles continuously entering the Earths atmosphere at incredible rates. When a proton collides with an atom in the Earths atmosphere, it can break apart that atom to produce elements in the form of neutrons, protons, and other particles. Those particles continue traveling toward the Earths surface, likely colliding with additional atoms on the way.

When entering the Earths surface, these particles quickly collide with the atoms around it, breaking apart more atoms in the upper 2 meters of a rock. Some elements are only produced by interaction with radiation. By measuring the amount of these elements you this now allows you to determine how long it has been exposed to the suns radiation.

When scientists used this method they found amazing results. It said that all the rock in the area was unearthed or eroded at the same time and that it happened 9,000 years ago. The stories behind the disasters

Two of the most devastating mega floods are the English Channel flood and the Texas mega flood. The floods literally tore the landscape they were in. The marks and scars are there to prove it but what really happened those days?

The mystery of the Texas mega flood started when a huge storm hit central Texas. Canyon lake reservoir flooded a huge part of the area. The water tore through the area, every 2 second enough water to fill an olympic swimming pool poured over. When the water subsided, a huge scar and mark was left onto the bedrock. The gorge was 23 feet deep and over a mile long. This was scientific proof. It showed that Mega Floods could still happen. It also proved that a flood could still happen on a huge scale. This means that one day or another a flood this devastating and powerful could rip apart what we see today.

The mystery of the english channel started when scientists researched the white cliffs of Dover. While doing research they made an amazing discovery. Buried inside the cliffs were things called bands of flint. These bands of flint going up and down the cliffs form a geological fingerprint into the cliffs. These geological fingerprints are one of a kind and they go through the whole cliff. The surprising thing is that both sides of the English Channel have the same geological fingerprint. This allows scientists to draw the conclusion that a huge mass of land once connected england to france. Scientists are puzzled mainly because they have yet to see a disaster with the power to rip apart the land bridge between France and England.

To find the answer to this mystery scientists scan the channels floor and they find something amazing. Deep at the channels bottom lays mountains and hills of rock carved by a violent force. A mega flood. Scientists now believe that a natural dam of chalk formed that held in melted water from the ice sheet over russia. When the dam broke it released almost half of its water. The land bridge was broken a now filled with water.
